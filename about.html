About Us

What we offer

Exibitions

ACL's widely acclaimed exhibitions cover the entire spectrum of the photographic medium, from early daguerreotypes, documentary images, and contemporary photography to multimedia installations.

Education

Serving more than 3,500 adult and teen students each year, ACL’s school offers photography education for every level, from courses and workshops to certificate and master’s degree programs.

Library

Comprised of over 20,000 books, artist files, and periodicals, ACL's reading library provides information and inspiration to the general public.

Public Programs

ACL's Public Programming at the Center for Visual Culture brings together diverse communities in public engagement to dialogue about ever transforming photographic and lens-based practices.
